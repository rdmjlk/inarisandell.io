Softspeed (2022)



Softspeed is a collaborative process between artists Hertta Kiiski, Inari Sandell, and Anniina Saksa — a shifting space for works-in-progress, thoughts, and conversation. The exhibition evolves over time, inviting visitors to move, listen, and sense. The works interact, shifting attention between fragility and force, intimacy and distance.

fast, soft, cold

Part of Ehkä ry’s XS Festival. Supported by Arts Promotion Centre Finland and The Finnish Art Society.
